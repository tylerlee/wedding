Our Wedding

The ceremony and reception will be taking place the evening of August 28th, in Telluride. We hope you are able to join us for a fun-filled evening of celebration!!

Event times will be updated in April, so check back for more details. Please see below for details on the beautiful venues!

Event attire is semi-formal. The ceremony will take place outdoors, so please check the weather prior to packing! Late August usually yields highs in the low 70s and lows around 40 overnight. So will be a nice break from the hot Texas summer!

Cocktail hour will follow the ceremony, on the deck at Gorrono Ranch. The reception will then be inside Gorrono, with access to the deck throughout the evening.
